T POKER

Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 12, 2026

T Poker is a private home-game poker management app. This policy explains what data the app handles, where it lives, and what control you have over it.

Guest mode — your data stays on your device

You can use T Poker without creating an account. Games you run in guest mode (players, buy-ins, cash-outs, settlements) are stored only on your device and are never sent to our servers. Deleting the app deletes this data. Player names you enter in guest games refer to people at your table and exist only in your device's local storage.

Account data

If you create an account, we store:

  • Identity: your username, email address, and a securely hashed password (we never store plain-text passwords). If you sign in with Google, we store your Google account identifier and email.
  • Game data: groups you belong to, sessions you play, buy-ins, cash-outs, settlements, hand records, session notes, and derived statistics (P&L, streaks, achievements).
  • Social data: group memberships, invitations, and in-app notifications.

What we don't do

  • We do not sell or share your data with third parties.
  • We do not run ads or advertising trackers.
  • We do not process real-money transactions — T Poker only records what you track. Settling up happens between you and your friends, outside the app.

Authentication & security

Sign-in uses short-lived access tokens with rotating refresh tokens. Tokens are stored in your device's secure storage (Keychain on iOS, Keystore on Android). Passwords are hashed with bcrypt. Refresh tokens are stored hashed on our servers.

Delete your account & data

In the app: Profile → Delete Account. This permanently removes your account and all associated personal data (profile, game records, group memberships, notifications, device tokens) from our servers, immediately.

Without the app (e.g. after uninstalling): email tayshofer05@gmail.com from the address on your account with the subject "Account deletion request". We verify ownership and delete the account within 30 days, with confirmation.

Guest-mode data never reaches our servers — remove it by deleting games in the app or uninstalling.

Data location

Account data is stored in a PostgreSQL database hosted on Railway. The web app is served via Vercel. Local guest data never leaves your device.

Children

T Poker is intended for adults organizing private home games and is not directed at children under 17.
